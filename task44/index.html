Famous Places in Darbhanga

Darbhanga Fort

The Darbhanga Fort is a historical fort that reflects the grandeur of the past. It is a must-visit for history enthusiasts.

Shyama Mai Temple

Shyama Mai Temple is another prominent temple in Darbhanga, dedicated to Goddess Kali. It is a popular pilgrimage site.

Lalit Narayan Mithila University

Lalit Narayan Mithila University is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Bihar, known for its academic excellence.
